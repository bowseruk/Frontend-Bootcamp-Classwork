Steps to make a sandwich

  1. Open the bread bag
  2. Remove two slices of bread on a clean surface
  3. Place one slice of bread with the surface that was connected to the second surface facing upwards
  4. Use a knife and skim the surface of butter to coat it
  5. Skim the knife against the loaf with enough pressure to coat, but not enough to damage the bread
  6. Repeat the step for the other slice on the surface that was connected to the first slice of bread
  7. Insert the filling on one of the layers
  8. Place the slice without filling butter side onto the filling side of the other slice
  9. eat
